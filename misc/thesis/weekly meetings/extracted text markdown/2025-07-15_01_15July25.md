## Slide 3

- Crown Matching & Merging

- Crowns from OM3 and OM4 were compared using an IoU threshold of 0.7

- If IoU(OM3[i], OM4[j]) > 0.7 → crowns were merged via geometric union

- The merged geometry replaced the original crowns in both OM3 and OM4

- Merging Crowns Based on IoU

## Slide 4

- If the union of OM4 overlaps is larger, maybe OM4 overestimated → show B−A.

## Slide 6

- Challenge in Multi-Temporal Alignment

- What I Did

- Then tried aligning OM5 to the already-aligned OM4 crowns.

- What Went Wrong
