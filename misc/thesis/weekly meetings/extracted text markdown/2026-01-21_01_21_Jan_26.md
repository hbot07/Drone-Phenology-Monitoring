## Slide 4

- Medoid

- Picks one of the existing per-OM crown polygons from the chain that minimizes a weighted sum of: normalized centroid distance,

- (1−IoU), and (1−area ratio) across all pairwise comparisons.

## Slide 5

- Intersection Core

- buffer each polygon outward by buffer_dist (tolerance for small misalignment),

- intersect all buffered polygons,

- buffer the intersection inward by -buffer_dist to get back to a core,

- keep the largest resulting polygon; if empty/tiny, fall back to consensus_medoid.

## Slide 6

- Union Shrink

- take the union of all polygons (then keep the largest component),

- set target_area = median area of the per-OM crowns,

- if the union is larger than target, shrink it via a negative buffer chosen (binary search) so the resulting area ≈ median.

## Slide 7

- Consensus Crown: Misalignment Problem

- Due to misalignment:

- we include different areas(in our example solar panel vs ground)

- In intersection based consensus crowns, we strip the crowns losing data and also get a weird crown shape

- In union based methods we also get weird shapes

- We need a more aggressive shifting/alignment algorithm to reduce these issues.

## Slide 8

- Crown Shifting/Alignment

- Earlier we based this completely on the crowns.

- This depends on crown detection quality, sensitive to missing/extra crowns and crown shape changes, and doesn’t use orthomosaic image evidence.

- New Shifting Algorithm: Image‑based Phase Correlation

- Phase cross‑correlation estimates the translation between two images by comparing their Fourier phase. It finds dx,dy that best aligns shared structures, and is also robust to overall brightness changes

## Slide 9

- Image‑based Phase Correlation

- Uses orthomosaic pixels (grayscale).

- Crop to overlapping area, resize to match.

- Phase cross‑correlation estimates translation (dx,dy)

- Apply cumulative shifts across consecutive OMs.

- The shift turned out to be similar +-2m for most of the orthomosaics, but for OM5 the difference was +9m,+3m

- This leads to lot of our OM5 crowns be tracked correctly from OM4 now and we get 9 more full chains!
