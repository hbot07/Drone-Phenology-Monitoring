## Slide 6

- Color indices

- GCC — Green Chromatic Coordinate

- GCC = G / (R + G + B)

- Proxy for leaf presence / chlorophyll activity

- Higher GCC → greener canopy

- Lower GCC → leaf loss, dryness, senescence

- RCC — Red Chromatic Coordinate

- RCC = R / (R + G + B)

- Veg Fraction

- vegetation fraction = (# vegetation pixels) / (valid pixels)

- Very sensitive to:

- leaf shedding, crown thinning, gaps in canopy

## Slide 7

- Texture / heterogeneity metrics

- These capture canopy structure, not color.

- When leaves fall:

- branches become visible

- shadows increase

- canopy becomes patchy

- Texture increases.

- Gray_std: Standard deviation of grayscale intensity.

- low → uniform canopy

- high → structural variation

- Gray_std_smooth, gray_entropy, energy

## Slide 32

- Classification of Errors

- MISSING_DETECTION

- A ground truth crown has no matching consensus crown with IoU ≥ threshold

- FALSE_POSITIVE

- A consensus crown has no matching ground truth crown with IoU ≥ threshold

- SPLIT_ERROR

- One ground truth crown matches multiple consensus crowns

- MERGE_ERROR

- Multiple ground truth crowns match a single consensus crown

- POOR_OVERLAP

- IoU < threshold BUT there is some overlap (IoU ≥ 0.15) AND the size difference is NOT significant (<30%)
