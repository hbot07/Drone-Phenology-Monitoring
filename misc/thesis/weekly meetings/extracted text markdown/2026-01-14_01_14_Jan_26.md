## Slide 2

- External GPS hardware

- External receivers pair via Bluetooth or USB

- provide sub-meter to centimeter-level accuracy using RTK/dual-frequency GNSS

- Phone GNSS: 3–10 meters

- Good external GNSS (no RTK): 0.3–1 meter

- GNSS + RTK: 1–3 cm

- RTK (Real-Time Kinematic): is a technique that improves GNSS accuracy.
