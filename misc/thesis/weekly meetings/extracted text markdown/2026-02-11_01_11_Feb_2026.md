## Slide 10

- Classification of Errors

- MISSING_DETECTION

- A ground truth crown has no matching consensus crown with IoU ≥ threshold

- FALSE_POSITIVE

- A consensus crown has no matching ground truth crown with IoU ≥ threshold

- SPLIT_ERROR

- One ground truth crown matches multiple consensus crowns

- MERGE_ERROR

- Multiple ground truth crowns match a single consensus crown

- POOR_OVERLAP

- IoU < threshold BUT there is some overlap (IoU ≥ 0.15) AND the size difference is NOT significant (<30%)
