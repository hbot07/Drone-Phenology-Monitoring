## Slide 2

- Objective

- Align tree crowns from two orthomosaic-derived crowns (OM3 → OM4), resolve missing crowns, and identify geometric mismatches.

## Slide 3

- Workflow Steps

- CRS Unification

- Reprojected both OM3 and OM4 crowns to match the CRS of the raster image sit_om4.tif.

- Affine Alignment (OM3 → OM4)

- Matched crowns via nearest neighbor centroid search (within 10 meters).

- Computed rigid affine transform (scale + rotation + translation).

- Applied it to OM3 → significantly improved spatial match.

IoU improved from ~30% → ~72%
 Transformed OM3 saved as: crowns3_aligned.gpkg

## Slide 4

- Crown Completion Logic

- A).

- Handle Completely Missing Crowns (IoU = 0)

- OM3-only crowns (not found in OM4) were copied to OM4 (origin = "from_om3")

- OM4-only crowns were similarly added to OM3 (origin = "from_om4")

Inverse affine transform applied to OM4 crowns when transferring to OM3
→ Ensures alignment correctness after full transformation

## Slide 5

- Crown Completion Logic

- B.)

- Partial Mismatches (IoU < 63%) —> A−B / B−A

- Computed crown difference geometries:

- OM3 crown − OM4 crown → added to OM4 as "om3_diff"

- OM4 crown − OM3 crown → added to OM3 as "om4_diff"

These are visualized as yellow and purple-outlined polygons in the plot
 Highlights incomplete overlaps or shape shifts between datasets
