## Slide 2

- Phase cross-correlation

- Estimates a pure translation between two images by finding the shift that best aligns their frequency content

- It’s a strong, fast baseline for global translational drift. We use low‑res grayscale orthomosaics

- If two images differ only by translation, then their Fourier transforms differ by a **linear phase ramp**.

- The peak of the inverse Fourier transform of the normalized cross-power spectrum gives the translation.
