## Slide 2

- Additional Params for Matching

- We see wrong matches in crowded regions. Use weighted combination of geometric and spatial features

- spatial_sim = max(0, 1 - (centroid_dist / max_dist))

- area_sim = min(area1, area2) / max(area1, area2)

- Compactness: (4 * π * area) / (perimeter^2) (how circular the shape is).

- Eccentricity: Ratio of minor to major axis of the minimum bounding rectangle.

- shape_sim = (compactness_sim + eccentricity_sim) / 2
