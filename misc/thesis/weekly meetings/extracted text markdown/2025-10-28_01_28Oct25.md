## Slide 12

- Detectree 2 - Stitching and Cleaning

- Stitching concatenates all per-tile detections and reconciles tile-boundary seams so the same crown detected in overlapping tiles isn‚Äôt double-counted.

- Cleaning De-duplicates and filters polygons using geometry overlap and confidence.

- If two crowns overlap more than a chosen IoU threshold (we used 0.7), they‚Äôre considered the same tree;

- Drops low-confidence polygons under the confidence threshold (we used 0.5).

- Returns valid, simplified geometries.
