## Slide 3

- What is DSM?

- DSM = Digital Surface Model

- Provides height of surface including trees/buildings

- Obtained using photogrammetry from RGB images

- No need for LiDAR – can be derived from multi-angle drone imagery

## Slide 4

- BalSAM – Proposed Method

- Builds on RSPrompter

- Uses a trainable DSM encoder to fuse elevation data with SAM inputs

- Result: Better tree crown segmentation, especially in structured plantations
