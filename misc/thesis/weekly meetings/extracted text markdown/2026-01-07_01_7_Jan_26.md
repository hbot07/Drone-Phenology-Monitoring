## Slide 5

- Working of algorithm

- For each candidate successor branch, look ahead W OMs and count whether at each OM the branches remain split (>=2 distinct nodes) or collapse to a single node.

- Compute split_support = number of OMs where branches remain split (or sum of branch counts) and single_support = number of OMs showing single continuity.

- Decide split wins when split_support > single_support
