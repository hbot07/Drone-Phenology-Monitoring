## Slide 3

- New idea

- For Many One

- Use 2 orthomosaic at a time, and after finding prediction from detectree2 apply the method For each index_right group (i.e., one c2 crown matched with many c1 crowns):

- Fetch all matching c1 geometries.

- Compute a unified geometry using unary_union.

- For each crown in the group:

- Calculate the IoU (Intersection over Union) between the unified shape and the crown.

- If all crowns have IoU ≥ 0.4 and the merged shape is smaller than the 95th area percentile:

- Accept the merge and store the union.

- Track the merged crown IDs.

- Else:

- Keep the crowns unchanged.

## Slide 5

- Overlap removal within same orthomosaic crowns

- Identify Candidates: Quickly find potentially overlapping pairs (using spatial indexing).

- Process in Passes: Repeatedly apply rules until no more significant changes occur:

- Remove Contained Fragments: If Crown A is small and fully inside Crown B, delete A.

- Merge Duplicates: If Crown A and Crown B have very high IoU (Intersection-over-Union, >90%), keep the larger one.

- Clip Partial Overlaps: If larger Crown A significantly overlaps smaller Crown B (e.g., >50% of B is covered), trim B by "subtracting" A's shape (B = B - A).
