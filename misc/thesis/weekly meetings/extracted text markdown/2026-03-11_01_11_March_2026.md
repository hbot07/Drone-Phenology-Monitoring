## Slide 7

- Leaf Shed/No Leaf Shed Signals

- Turn each tree’s multi-date signal trajectory into

- A continuous deciduousness score in [0,1]

- A binary class: deciduous vs evergreen

- A per-date phenophase label for every tree across all orthomosaics

- We use

- veg_fraction_hsv as the primary leaf-presence signal.

- gcc_mean as a secondary greenness signal.

- laplacian_var as a texture-structure support signal.

## Slide 8

- Leaf Shed/No Leaf Shed Signals

- For each tree, the we compute four component scores:

- s_veg_amp: how large the seasonal swing is in vegetation fraction.

- s_depth: how deep the minimum goes relative to a minimum-vegetation threshold.

- s_gcc_amp: how much GCC changes seasonally.

- s_tex: how much texture changes seasonally.

- DS=0.35s_veg_amp+ 0.30s_depth+ 0.25s_gcc_amp+ 0.10s_tex

- ​is_deciduous=(DS≥0.40)
