## Slide 4

- Detectree2

- The large orthomosaic was split into 45x45 pixel tiles with a 20-pixel buffer.

- This ensures smaller sections are analyzed for better detection accuracy.

- Pre trained model 230103_randresize_full.pth

- Detectree2 provides a predict_on_data function

- Converted tile-based predictions into geo-referenced outputs (GeoJSON)

- Filtered overlapping crowns by selecting the most confident detections.

- Saved the processed tree crowns as a GeoPackage file (.gpkg).
